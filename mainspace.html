What Mainspace is all about?

Mainspace is all about making users objective about the information we encounter everyday. We do use news and data driven research, but we would also like people to be more aware of it, which is why we are making this about you.

We intend to work on building a better news environment - news which you know which can be trusted, news which you know is important and news which will you bring to light the statistics, but we aren't just about news.

We are all about research and how everyone should be a part of it, because we know it's important for everyone to know. We also intend to inspire research in fields we know, we must grow in.

And lastly and definitely not the least, we want you to be aware of how other organizations are shaping our society, and thus make it our mission to make sure you stay informed.
