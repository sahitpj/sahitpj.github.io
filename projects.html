Agribot

India is an Argo-based economy and proper information is key to optimal agricultural growth and output. In order to answer the query of the farmer we build an agricultural chatbot based on the dataset from Kisan Call Center. The system is robust enough to answer queries related to weather, market rates, plant protection and government schemes. This system is available 24*7 and can be accessed through any electronic device.

Project Team - Naman Jain, Pranjali Jain, Pratik Kayal, P Jayakrishna Sahit, Soham Pachpande
